Our Journey

What began as a child’s innocent observation that all packaging is “trash in the making” has since grown into a deep, socially-conscious consumer mega-trend.

WE COMMITTED OURSELVES TO FIND A BETTER WAY.
Our goal is 100% backyard compostability. We are on the right track and making great progress. We’d like you to join us, help us, challenge us, work with us and be part of the movement for the greater cause. Currently, Paper Water Bottle products are 100% recyclable, 98% landfill biodegradable* and 65% compostable.
